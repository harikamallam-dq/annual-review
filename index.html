My Offer as Team Lead
What I plan to bring —
and deliver — every single day.
Mentoring & 1:1s — Individual growth plans, skill-sharing, structured coaching for every team member.
Project planning — Task assignment, timelines, progress tracking, deadline ownership across concurrent engagements.
QA ownership — Reviewing every deliverable before it goes to the client. Catching issues — false positives, missed criteria — before they become someone else's problem.
Client communication — Keeping clients updated, handling queries, and managing anything that needs escalating — calmly and professionally.
Keeping leadership in the loop — Flagging risks early, sharing what's happening on the ground, and not waiting until something is already a problem.
Workload distribution — Making sure the right people are on the right work, keeping an eye on who has capacity and who needs support.
Audits & VPATs — Still doing the work, not just overseeing it. Reviewing for Section 508 and EN 301 549 compliance and making sure nothing slips through.
Building shared resources — Writing things down so the team doesn't have to rely on one person knowing something. Checklists, notes, process docs that actually get used.
Noticing patterns — When the same issue keeps coming up across different projects, saying so — and thinking about how to prevent it rather than just fixing it each time.
Connecting the dots — Being the link between what the team is experiencing day to day and what leadership needs to make decisions. Not waiting to be asked.
If all of this sounds like just doing my job — that's exactly the point. I'm not trying to do everything at once. One thing, done fully, with maximum impact. That's my approach to this role right now.
